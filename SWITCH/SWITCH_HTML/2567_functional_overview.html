NI PXI-2567

The NI PXI-2567 is a 64-channel general-purpose relay driver module for the PXI platform. The NI PXI-2567 is designed to handle up to 0.6 A drive current and up to 50 V drive voltage. The NI PXI-2567 also has two sources available for driving relays.

Operation Modes

The following table lists the supported topology of the NI PXI-2567 and possible operation modes.

Note  Using two channels per relay, the NI PXI-2567 can also control 32 two-coil latching relays.
