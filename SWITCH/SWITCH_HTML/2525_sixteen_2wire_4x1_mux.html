NI PXIe-2525 2-Wire Sixteen 4×1 Multiplexer Topology

The following figure represents the NI PXIe-2525 (NI 2525) in the 2-wire sixteen 4×1 multiplexer topology.

Making a Connection

When using the NI 2525 as a 2-wire sixteen 4×1 multiplexer, the positive leads of the first bank (ch0+ through ch3+) route to com0+, and the negative leads of the first bank (ch0– through ch3–) route to com0–. The pair com0+ and com0– is addressed collectively as com0 in software. All other banks follow a similar routing scheme.

The immediate operation command (the niSwitch Connect Channels VI or the niSwitch_Connect function), with parameters ch2 and com0, result in the following connections:

signal connected to ch2+ is routed to com0+

signal connected to ch2– is routed to com0–
