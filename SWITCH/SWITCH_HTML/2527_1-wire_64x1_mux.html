NI PXI/PXIe-2527 1-Wire 64×1 Multiplexer Topology

Connect to the pins of the NI TB-2627 terminal block to use the NI PXI/PXIe-2527 (NI 2527) as a 1-wire 64×1 multiplexer. In this topology, all channel terminals (CH0 through CH63) route to COM0+. The 1WREF0 lead is always connected to COM0–. COM0+ is addressed as com0 in software.
