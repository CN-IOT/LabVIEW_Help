NI PXI/PXIe-2575

The NI PXI/PXIe-2575 (NI 2575) is a 196×1 multiplexer relay module for the PXI or PXI Express platform. The NI 2575 is composed of 99 DPDT relays. These relays are configured in hardware as 98 DPST relays and one DPDT relay.

For certain applications, you may need to increase the default settling time. Refer to Adding Additional Settling Time for more information.

Note  Switching inductive loads (for example, motors and solenoids) can produce high voltage transients in excess of the module's rated voltage. Without additional protection, these transients can interfere with module operation and impact relay life. For more information about transient suppression, refer to Protecting NI Switch Products when Switching Inductive Loads.

Operation Modes

The following table lists the supported topology of the NI 2575 and possible operation modes.

2-Wire 98×1 Multiplexer 2575/2-Wire 98x1 Mux
(NISWITCH_TOPOLOGY_2575_2_WIRE_98X1_MUX)
2-Wire 95×1 Multiplexer 2575/2-Wire 95x1 Mux
(NISWITCH_TOPOLOGY_2575_2_WIRE_95X1_MUX)
Note  When using either the SH200LFH-4xDB50F-S or SH200LFH-BARE WIRE cable with the NI 2575 in the 2-wire 98×1 topology, CH95, CH96, and CH97 will have lower RF performance than the other 95 channels because they are not in twisted pairs in the cable. To avoid using these channels, NI-SWITCH has support for a 2-wire 95×1 topology that does not include CH95, CH96, and CH97.
